    About me

    3D Modeler/Web Developer

    Hello and welcome to my page. My name is Rachel I attended The Los Angeles Film School in 2019 where I studied VFX and Animation. I graduated as an honors student in August 2022. I'm expanding my artistic studies here at the PDX code guild while primarily focusing on frontend design. My interest tends to shift regularly, allowing me to jump from one task to another, trying to learn many areas in the 3D production pipeline. Currently, my main focus is asset modeling, texturing, and UI design. As for my other interest, I enjoy playing with the particle systems in Blender, watching stop-motion films, and compositing.
