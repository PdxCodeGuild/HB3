Biography - Ernest Shackleton

15 February 1874 - 5 January 1922

Introduction

Sir Ernest Henry Shackleton was an Anglo-Irish explorer of Antartica who attempted to reach the South Pole. He is best known as a polar explorer who was associated with four expeditions exploring Antartica, particularly the Trans-Antartic (Endurance) Expedition (1914-1916) that he led, which although unsuccessful, became famous as a tale of remarkable perseverance and survival. He died during his fourth expedition in 1920 and is buried on the island of South Georgia in the South Atlantic Ocean.
