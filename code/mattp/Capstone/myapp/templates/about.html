About Me

I am a former Sailor, who has always had a passion for the ocean. I would love to find ways to help maintain fish species we use for seafood, as well as, make sure people continue to be able to enjoy their local favorites!

The hope for this project is to give people a place where they can figure out local sources of seafood, and to provide them with nutritional and conservation info about them.

If you would like more information about the project, or to request a certain zipcode or fish be added (sooner rather than later), please feel free to use the Contact section, to let me know!

Thank you!
